English autobiography

My name is Lu, Jia-Zhen. I am a college student in National Tsing Hua Universtiy in Taiwan now with a major in Foreign Languages and Literature. I was born in Nantou, a tranquil and peaceful place. That’s why I admire serenity and nature.
I am a person who’s concerned about climate change as a global issue. I used to work as an intern in the “Center for Corporate Sustainability” of “Taiwan Institute for Sustainable Energy” for two months in 2020, to help the publicity of “Global Corporate Sustainability Forum” and also learn about Corporate Social Responsibility and “Sustainable Development Goals”.
Besides, during my time in college, I also take courses in the teaching program. In 2019, I joined “2K19 NTUST x Project LET’S GO! Summer program in Taiwan” to work with volunteer teachers around the world to teach English in the remote mountain area in Kaohsiung, Xiaolin. And now, I am working as an English teacher in a cram school in my free time. My students are second and third grade in elementary school.

自我介紹

我喜歡接觸不同的事物，因為在這樣的過程中，能感受到自己對於人類或世界有更多、更深的了解，總覺得世界如此的豐富，很希望多看一點，多知道一點,這也是我為什麼喜歡文學課的原因，大學期間，在外文系的修課過程中，我修了很多的文學課，藉由閱讀不同時代的文學，擴展對於人類與世界的認識。
我也非常關心環境方面的議題，除了在學校修習永續方面的課程，2020年暑假，我到台灣永續能源研究基金會實習，協助宣傳全球企業永續論壇，並學習企業社會責任與全球永續發展目標的相關知識。
除此之外，我也修習小學師資類科教育學程，並曾參加2019 PLG幸福小林國際雙語夏令營，與世界各地的志工老師一起到高雄的偏遠山區小林村教授英文。目前，我也在課餘的時間到補習班教授小學的英語課程。
